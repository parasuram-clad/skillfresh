Campus Ready Program

Educate. Evaluate. Empower.

What is Campus Ready?

Campus Ready is a 100-Day Challenge designed to transform students into industry-ready professionals. Through workshops, skill evaluations, and corporate connections, we empower students to excel in placements and beyond.

  • 2-day industry-led workshop
  • Daily skill assessments for 100 days
  • Real-time leaderboard & feedback
  • Direct recruiter connections
The Journey

How Campus Ready Works

Step 01
Career Workshop
  1. 2 Days

An offline program to guide students in Placement Preparation by industry experts.

Step 02
Skill Evaluation
  1. 105 Days

A daily skill assessment with predefined topics and a real-time leaderboard.

Step 03
Corporate Connect
  1. 1 Year

Companies conduct interviews and offer internships and jobs to the right candidates.
